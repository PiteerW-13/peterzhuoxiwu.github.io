Preface - Dreams of Astronomy Lovers

As a seasoned astronomy enthusiast, I often find myself lost in fantasies of the breathtaking starry sky. One tranquil summer night, I stood alone by the windowsill, nestled against the frame of my telescope, pondering the enigmatic secrets of the cosmos.

Lost in my musings, I failed to notice the lateness of the hour and soon succumbed to drowsiness, drifting into slumber without realizing it.

When I finally awoke, I found myself aboard a spacecraft, my heart aflutter with curiosity and astonishment. Before I could fully comprehend my surroundings, a captivating voice resonated in my ears: "Greetings, sir. I am PETE, an artificial intelligence, to be precise, an educated robot designed to promote the wonders of astrophysics and astronomy..."

Before I could respond, drowsiness overcame me once more, and I could only vaguely sense the spaceship accelerating toward an unfamiliar destination.
