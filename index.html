Welcome

Hi here!!! I am Peter Zhuo Xi Wu, a first year Physics and Data Science student at Constructor University. I am originally from China. I am a dedicated explorer of the cosmos with a passion for unraveling the mysteries of the universe. Academically, my research interest lies in compact objects and the influence of the gravitational waves from astrophysics, and the galatical formation and evolution from cosmology, as well as the computational skills needed to investigate them.

With a deep-rooted curiosity for all things celestial, I have spent years immersed in the study of astronomy and astrophysics, translating complex scientific concepts into accessible language for readers of all backgrounds. Now, I have bridged cultures and perspectives, drawing inspiration from diverse experiences and scientific communities worldwide. Beyond academic pursuits, I have been actively involved in fostering cross-cultural exchange, organizing events, and leading presentations to share my knowledge and inspire others.

In my personal writing, PETE-A Voyage Through the Space, I invite readers from the perspective of PETE to journey through the cosmos, exploring everything from the birth of stars to the intricate dance of galaxies. So from now on, immerse yourself in the vastness of the universe, let’s follow PETE, traveling in space!
