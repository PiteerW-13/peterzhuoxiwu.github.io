Research Interests

My academic interests focus on astrophysics and cosmology, with particular emphasis on compact objects, galaxy formation, and gravitational-wave-related phenomena. I am especially interested in combining theoretical modeling with computational techniques to explore the underlying physical mechanisms governing the universe.
